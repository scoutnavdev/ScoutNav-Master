Privacy

Privacy is a top priority for me- espescially in a world where increasingly businesses are tracking indivuduals. I take industry-standard steps to secure you (like SSL), as well as some I'm not going to talk about for obvious reasons.

So... What is tracked?

You are recorded as a "user", to let me know someone visited a page on my system. In addition, your basic location is gathered from your IP address (country, nothing else.) It's that simple. I don't know and frankly don't care about anything else. No cookies except for password tokens, no monitoring. The only exception to this is if you're visiting using a strange or uncommon method (eg, TOR) you will be prompted with a CAPCHTA.

What I don't have control over

I cannot control tracking from external sites or apps (eg. Google Maps, Waze) They do their own things, and their privacy policy can be viewed on they're websites.
